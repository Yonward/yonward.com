I. Information We Collect

 

We collect both “Personal Information” and “Non-Personal Information.” Personal Information is information that you submit to us either through the Site or the App or by registering for Yonward through another social networking site (such as Facebook), and that is personally identifiable (i.e. can be linked to a specific individual) such as your name, email address and telephone number. “Non-Personal Information” includes information that cannot be used to personally identify you, such as anonymous usage data, general demographic information, geo-location data, platform types, number of clicks.

 

We collect various types of this information, including the following:

 

1. INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE US BY REGISTERING FOR A YONWARD ACCOUNT OR DOWNLOADING THE APP

 

Visitors may access and use the Service without disclosing most Personal Information. In addition, we allow users to create their own profile through the App or the Site to provide users with a better and more customized experience; when a user creates a profile, we collect Personal Information that users voluntarily provide through the registration process.

 

Users can create a profile in two ways. First, users can register directly through the Site or on the App. When registering in this way, we collect a user’s name and email address, and offer the user the option of additionally providing their mailing address and gender. Second, users can register to create a Yonward profile by connecting through a third party social networking site (“SNS”) that already contains the user’s personal information, such as Facebook. The information you allow Yonward to access varies by SNS platform, and is affected by the privacy settings you establish at the respective SNS. For example, Yonward may access and store some or all of the following information, as allowed by you, the SNS, and your preferences:

• Your first and last name

• Your profile picture or its URL

• The login email you provided to that SNS when you registered

• The content you posted to that SNS, such as likes and comments, and other information.

• Profile information such as your hometown, gender, interests and activities, and other information.

 

By registering a Yonward profile through an SNS, you authorize Yonward to collect, store, and use any and all information you allow the SNS to provide to Yonward through that SNS’s application programming interface (which is the program that allows an SNS and Yonward to share information). Your agreement with Yonward and your acceptance of this Privacy Policy occurs when you accept or allow an SNS to register you for a Yonward account.

 

You have the option to use Yonward’ “Find friends” feature to locate other people with Yonward accounts either through (i) your contacts list, or (ii) third-party social networking sites. If you choose to find friends through your device’s contacts list, then Yonward will access your contacts list and determine whether or not your contacts are using Yonward. If you choose to find friends through a third-party social media site, then you will be prompted to set up a link to the third-party service and you understand that any information that such service may provide to Yonward will be governed by this Privacy Policy.

 

If you invite someone to the Yonward service through the “Invite Friends” feature, you can select a person directly from your contacts list and send a text or email from your personal account. In doing so, you understand and agree that you are responsible for any charges that apply to communications sent from you device, and because this invitation is coming directly from your personal account, Yonward does not have access to or control this communication.

 

2. INFORMATION COLLECTED VIA TECHNOLOGY

 

In addition to the personal information you voluntarily provide when you register for the Site or download the App, in an effort to improve the quality of Yonward’s service, we track information provided to us when you use the App or view the Site, such as the website you came from (known as the “referring URL”), the type of browser you use, the time and date of access, and other information that does not personally identify you. We do so by sending one or more cookies or similar persistent identifiers (such as universally unique identifiers) to your computer or mobile device that uniquely identifies your browser or device. A cookie is a small text file composed of alphanumeric letters, which includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to a user’s browser from our servers and is stored on the user’s computer hard drive. Doing so allows us to collect Non-Personal Information about our users and keep a record of user preferences when utilizing the Services, both on an individual basis and in the aggregate.

 

For example, Yonward may compile the the number of times a user:

• Views their profile

• Clicks on the map or views a beacon

• Views a guide or beacon

• Clicks through to other sites linked to Yonward

• Views each guide or beacon

• Shares a guide or beacon through a social network.

 

Yonward may use both persistent and session cookies; persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your session and until you delete them, while session cookies expire when you close your browser. For example, we store a persistent cookie to track your last known location on the Site or App map. This way, when you return to the map, the map can refresh at exactly the same place as it was when you left.

 

3. GEOLOCATION DATA COLLECTED

 

If you use the Services through the App, we will collect your geo-location information in order to provide a better Yonward experience that incorporates information about your location and surroundings. We will not share this information with third parties and will only use this information for the sole purpose of customizing your Yonward experience. You may at any time disable the collection of Services to track your location by turning this off at the device level.
